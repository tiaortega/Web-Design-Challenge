Max Temperature

The City Latitude vs Max Temperature (01/05/17) data is observed as expected increase in temperature as one approaches the equator. The equator is at the 0 degree latitude. Due to the position of the earth at this time of the year, the southern hemisphere is indeed at higher temperatures than the northern hemisphere.
